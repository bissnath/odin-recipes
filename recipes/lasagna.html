Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is the ultimate comfort food, featuring layers of tender pasta, rich and hearty meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and gooey melted cheese. Perfectly seasoned with Italian herbs, this dish is baked to golden perfection, making it a family favorite for weeknight dinners or special occasions.

Each bite delivers a delightful blend of flavors and textures—savory ground beef, tangy tomato sauce, and velvety cheeses all coming together in one satisfying dish. Whether you’re serving it fresh out of the oven or reheating leftovers, this lasagna is sure to be a crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain any excess grease.
  4. Add the marinara sauce to the skillet with the beef and simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, egg, garlic powder, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  6. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  7. Layer 3-4 lasagna noodles over the sauce, slightly overlapping.
  8. Spread a layer of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, followed by a layer of meat sauce, and sprinkle with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  9. Repeat the layering process until all ingredients are used, finishing with a generous layer of cheese on top.
  10. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  11. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 20 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
  12. Let the lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving.
